# Rumble or All-In-One

The Rumble or All-In-One Design Sprint method is useful when there is more than one winning Solution Sketch.

* **Time:** Variable
* **Activity:** Group
* **Output:** Decision to combine into one prototype or run competing prototypes.
* **Source:** Core Method

### Directions

1. Decide as a group if you want to combine the winners into a single prototype (All-In-One) or develop two different ideas and test them against each other (Rumble). It’s often possible to combine a number of ideas into one prototype.
2. If you choose a Rumble, you will need to consider how to present the two opposing ideas to your users. Will you need to create an alternate brand/product to make the prototype believable? Allow extra time for writing your Validation Interview script.
