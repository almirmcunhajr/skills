# Empathy Building Exercises

Empathy Building Exercises are a Design Sprint method to get the team further into the user's mindset by using the product on which the Design Sprint focuses. This helps the team develop ideas that better meet the user's needs. For example, if the Design Sprint is for a mobile app, the team might use the app to complete a specific task. Then, the team reflects on their experience in writing, paying particular attention to any pain points encountered, as well as any moments of delight.

* **Time:** 30-50 mins
* **Activity:** Group
* **Output:** Observations, pain points, and moments of delight.

### Directions

1. Choose a critical task for your product.
2. Set aside 30 min.
3. Break into groups of 2 or 3 people.
4. Complete the task and record observations.
5. Share all observations with the larger group.
