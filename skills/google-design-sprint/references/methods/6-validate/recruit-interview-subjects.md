# Recruit Interview Subjects

Identifying who needs to be interviewed is essential. Define and isolate your target persona to begin your search. Some teams have dedicated researchers or partners that handle recruiting for them. If you don’t have a dedicated resource, you’ll need to personally locate your testers. This ensures you recruit qualified interview subjects, disqualify “professional” survey takers, and create targeted screener and ideal user types to interview.

* **Time:** 8 hours
* **Activity:** Individual
* **Output:** Recruited target participants.

### Directions

1. Review Sprint Questions, Goal, and Target.
2. List out disqualifying attributes and qualities.
3. List out qualifying attributes.
4. Write a screener containing questions targeting your qualifying and disqualifying attributes.
5. Publish screener to respondent.io or similar service.
6. Make an interview and “floater” schedule.
7. Review & invite participants.
8. Review & invite floaters.
