# Plan the Interview

The official Sprint Moderator Guide helps you get the most out of your 5-Act user interviews. When testing with 5 users, it’s critical that you run the interviews properly. This template is simple to use: open it and simply make a copy and rename it. Then follow the steps included to set it up for your interview. This saves time by using a prepared template for interviews, documents the key questions and target of the sprint for reference, structures the interview effectively by outlining your prototype’s click path, and unlocks the interview subject’s insights with proven context questions.

* **Time:** 60 mins
* **Activity:** Individual
* **Output:** Moderator guide and interview plan.
* **Source:** Voltage Control

### Directions

1. Download the guide.
2. Clone the guide.
3. Customize the guide for your test.
4. Print and study the guide.
5. Follow the guide when running your interview.
