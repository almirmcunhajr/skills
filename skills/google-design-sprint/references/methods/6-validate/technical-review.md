# Technical Review

Similar to the Stakeholder Review, the Technical Review is a Design Sprint method to secure buy-in from the engineer or technical lead for your product. It is ideal for technical stakeholders to participate in the entire Design Sprint, though it is not always possible. In this scenario, invite the engineer or the technical lead to join the Design Sprint for thirty minutes to provide feedback on the prototypes or early sketched concepts specifically for feasibility. If you have created ideas for new technology or in a new space for which you need a technical expert, you can set up a review with one from the appropriate industry.

* **Time:** 20 mins
* **Activity:** Group
* **Output:** Feasibility feedback, risks, and constraints.

### Directions

1. Schedule time in advance so that your engineer/expert can fit it into their calendar.
2. Invite them to come in person and participate for User Interviews or Usability Studies.
3. Consider creating a presentation of the prototypes and user feedback in advance to help make the review go smoothly.
