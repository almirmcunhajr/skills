# Stakeholder Review

The Stakeholder Review is a Design Sprint method used to confirm buy-in on the prototype from key stakeholders. It is ideal for the key stakeholders to participate in the entire Design Sprint, though it is not always possible. In this scenario, invite the stakeholders to join the Design Sprint for thirty minutes to provide feedback on the prototypes or early sketched concepts.

* **Time:** 30 mins
* **Activity:** Group
* **Output:** Buy-in, concerns, and stakeholder feedback.

### Directions

1. Schedule the review session as early as possible to make sure your stakeholder can fit it into their calendar.
2. If you are scheduling the review at the end of the Sprint, schedule it after User Sessions so you can share the results and help the stakeholder understand the user feedback.
3. Consider creating a presentation of the prototypes and user feedback in advance to help make the review go smoothly.
